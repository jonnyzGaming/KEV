K.E.V (kill every vegetable) is a 2d platform game where you play as kev the carrot. You will need to go on a quest to save your kids from an evil potato and no vegetable will stand in your way!

Features:
-Collect carrots to spend with ROY(aka the guy) on several weapon upgrades and much more!
-Talk-able npcs who can assist you in your adventure.
-Lots of enemies to kill and satisfy your need for vengeance.
-Amazing 16 bit character animations and graphics.
-Hidden areas and optional side quests.

K.E.V is a java game still in development.
